Find Brands

keeping fashion fun and sustainable

T A L A

Launched by youtube sensation and instgram influencer Grave Beverley, this clothing brand has been launched with sustainability being it's fundamental foundations. Already 92% of the products are produced from upcycled matierals with 100% plantable paper tags and recycable packaging. Tala is the new, on trend brand for affordable sustainable fashion.

fun fact: you can even plant the tag and watch it grow.

P A T A G O N I A

From their first day of establishment, the brand has centralised around the planet’s best interests. Making clothes from recycled materials to advertising customers to not buy a new Patagonia jacket, but to repair an old one instead. Their new 'recrafted' range features unqiuely remade in California from an array of deconstructed and repurposed matierals of various shades - making upcycling fashionable.

although sustainable, these clothes don't come at a low price.

B I T E

Originated in Stockholm, the brand centralises around their combined passion for sustainability. With a mixture of organic fabrics, the company promote the idea of timeless clothes that are made to last as the minimalist label comprises of twenty fixed styles of which are only updated seasonally. Most of the pieces are colours of navy, oatmeal and grey with the occasional orange. This allows for flexibility in style throughout the seasons. However, BITE does fall under the luxrious label and you will have to invest big amounts for such clothes.

fun fact: BITE is an acronym for By Independent Thinkers for Environment Progress.

P A C T

For a more affordable brand but with the same values, Pact is the perfect option for men’s, women’s and children’s apparel; with a focus on super-soft basics. The Garments are GOTS certified organic and fair trade. With a high-quality standard, and affordable price point – a purchase from Pact would be the ideal place to start in sustainability.

"from fast fashion, to ethical fashion"

K O M O D O

Komodo is an organic and ethical clothing brand from the UK with some seriously fun style! I love that Komodo is willing to go beyond the typical “ethical fashion box” of neutrals and basics with funky prints, unique designs portrayed with artsy, editorial style imagery on their website.

perfect for men and women.
